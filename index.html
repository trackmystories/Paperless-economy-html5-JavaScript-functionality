January 1st, 1983 is a date that changed the world forever. On this date the World-Wide-Web was created by a computer scientist name Tim Berners-Lee. and the world has transformed into a far smaller place to interact in.

The creation of the internet brought in a new kind of renaissance, Art is being expressed like never before and that very expression was evolving as technology evolved and continues to evolve to this day. Where things seem archaic just 10 years ago on how content looked essentially perceived.
